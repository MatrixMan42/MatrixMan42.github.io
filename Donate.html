Here at Greenhouse Awareness Australia, we want to support as many projects that promise to help clean up Australia and help reduce Greenhouse emissions as we can.

However, to do this we need the appropriate funds to make this happen.
This is where you come in!

Over the last 7 months, we've raised over $12,000 and managed to support 3 different clean projects.
As a result of these efforts, we've managed to:
Set up over 25 public bins along Misery Beach, reducing monthly litter intake by 30%.
Cleaned up the junk polluting the Cooks River, and put up signs around the river asking people not to discard their trash around it.
We've pruned many trees and made firebreaks in several forests/vegetetated areas around Victoria, helping to reduce the severity of bushfires in the area.
