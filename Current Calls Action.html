Helping out with the community

Greenhouse Awareness Australia hosts a variety of events around the country to help clean up litter, and reduce greenhouse emissions.
While there are a few monthly and annual events that GAA puts on, there's a number of schools, charities, and businesses that also host events.

Clean up Misery Beach

Our annual campaign to clean up the litter in Misery Beach. Join dozens of people who want the beach to stay tourist friendly.

Location: Misery Beach, WA

Next Date: 8th March, 2024 (Annualy)

Time: 8:30am - 2:00pm

Planting 5,000 Trees

Join a community of people who are trying to restore forest lands along Adelaides farmlands.

Location: 12 Tyre Road, Adelaide

Date: 12th April, 2024

Time: 10:00am - 6:00pm

Clean up Queen River

The Queen's river has been polluted by mining runoff, and we're working on a solution to fix it!

Location: Queen River, Tasmania

Next Date: 20th Febuary, 2024

Time: 9:00am - 3:00pm

Consider donating

Not able to be there in person but still want to help out? That's ok!
Every dollar that's donated can help a huge amount with each of these cleanup projects.
For instance, donating just one dollar can help to plant over 10 trees, or help to buy supplies for other cleanup projects.

About
