Greenhouse Awareness Australia

Here at GAA, we care about raising awareness about climate change and the effects that global warming is having on Australia's environment. We are also interested in seeing how every day lifestyle decisions are effecting the environment, as well as its impact on the local wildlife.

We are also interested in maintaining and proecting the environment, by hosting different programs that aim to help reduce waste and improve recycling, cleaning up litter, fixing unnatural environmental hazards, and minimising Australia's carbon footprint.
